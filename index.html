WHAT WE DO?

MicroSave is a diversified development consultancy that works to better the lives of low-income people.

Established in 1996 as a joint UNCD- F/DFID project in East and Southern Africa,today we have offices and staff in 34 countries around the world with headquaters in Lucknow,India.
